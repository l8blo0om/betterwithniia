The Course

5 Modules. Sequenced with intention.

Each module builds on the last — release before activation, structure before movement, foundation before integration.

01 Foundation

The Map of Your Face

Before you can release anything, you need to understand the architecture underneath. Module 1 covers facial and cranial anatomy — the muscles, fascia, and bones that determine how your face looks and moves.

  • Facial muscle anatomy for non-clinicians
  • Understanding fascia and why it grips
  • The connection between posture and the face
  • Your personal facial pattern assessment
02 Cranial & Jaw Release

Skull, Jaw & Cranial Decompression

Module 2 addresses the jaw, temporomandibular joint, and cranial fascia — the structural roots of tension that show up on the surface as lines, asymmetry, and fatigue.

  • Masseter and temporalis release
  • Jaw decompression technique
  • Cranial fascia unwinding
  • Trigger point work with ball tools

03 Neck & Décolletage

The Platysma Protocol

The platysma is the thin muscle of the neck that most women feel pulling — and most practitioners ignore. Module 3 addresses the front-of-body tension that ages the lower face and neck faster than almost anything else.

  • Platysma anatomy and release sequence
  • Front-of-body lengthening stretches
  • Neck and décolletage fascial work
  • Throat and voice release techniques
04 Activation & Toning

Face Yoga & Facial Exercises

Now that the tissue is released and the fascia is mobile, Module 4 introduces targeted facial exercises in the correct sequence — activation after release. This is what makes the results last.

  • Face yoga fundamentals
  • Cheek, brow, and eye activation
  • Lip and perioral toning
  • Symmetry correction exercises
05 Integration

Your Daily Ageless Practice

The final module brings everything together into a sustainable daily ritual you can actually maintain. You'll leave with a personalized 10-minute morning protocol and a longer weekly practice.

  • The 10-minute morning face sequence
  • Weekly deep release protocol
  • Breathing and nervous system integration
  • Long-term maintenance and progression
  • Recommended tools and affiliate links
