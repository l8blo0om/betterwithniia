The Method

What is Gyrotonic®?

A holistic movement method that combines principles of yoga, dance, tai chi, and swimming. It emphasizes three-dimensional, circular movements to stretch and strengthen the body while mobilizing the spine and joints.

Core Principles

Three-Dimensional Movement

Circular, spiraling, and undulating patterns — not the linear motions most exercise relies on.

Continuous Flow

Movements transition seamlessly. No holding positions, no stopping — just sustained, intelligent motion.

Breath Synchronization

Each movement correlates to a specific breathing pattern, stimulating cardiovascular health from within.

Spinal Mobilization

Arching, curling, twisting — the spine is treated as the centerpiece of every session.

Key Benefits

Joint Decompression

Rotational movements open joint spaces, reducing friction and chronic pain.

Core Strength

Stabilizes the deep abdominal and pelvic muscles — the foundation everything else builds on.

Improved Posture

Balances muscle groups to realign the spine and shoulders without force.

Athletic Performance

Enhances agility and range of motion — used by dancers, golfers, swimmers, and elite athletes.

Gyrotonic® vs. Pilates

Equipment

Pilates uses springs, flat tables, and precise resistance. Gyrotonic® uses weights, pulleys, and rotating discs.

Motion

Pilates focuses on linear, two-dimensional movement. Gyrotonic® prioritizes three-dimensional spirals.

At Get Better with Niia, sessions blend both methods — giving you the benefits of each, tailored to your body.
